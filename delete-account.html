Delete Your Account

App: ChowSA · Developer: ChowSA (Cape Town, South Africa)

You can delete your ChowSA account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways: directly inside the app (fastest), or by emailing us if you've lost access.

Method 1 — Inside the app (recommended)

1
Open ChowSA Sign in to the account you want to delete.
2
Tap Profile (bottom-right) Then the gear icon for Settings.
3
Privacy & Data Settings Tap "Erase My Data" at the bottom of that section.
4
Confirm A dialog asks you to confirm. Once you tap Delete, your account is queued for permanent removal.

Method 2 — By email (if you've lost access)

If you can't sign in, email us from the address linked to your account:

Email Deletion Request →

Include your registered email and (if you remember it) your ChowSA handle. We'll confirm receipt within 2 working days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar, handle, bio).
  • All recipes, pantry items, shopping lists, and meal plans you created.
  • All community posts, comments, and channel messages you sent.
  • Your friends list and shared assets.
  • Push notification token.
  • Any active Pro subscription is cancelled (no further billing).

What we keep

  • Anonymous aggregate data (e.g. "how many users use load-shedding mode") — no longer linked to you.
  • Payment records required by SA tax law (kept for 5 years, per SARS), but no card details — we never had those.
  • Abuse logs for accounts banned for serious community violations (so the same user can't immediately re-register).

How long it takes

Deletion completes within 7 days of your request, in line with POPIA. You'll receive a confirmation email when it's done. After that, the deletion is permanent — we can't recover the account.
