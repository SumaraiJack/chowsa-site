Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ChowSA ("we", "us", "the app") respects your privacy and complies with the South African Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and Google Play's Data Safety requirements. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how to control it.

What we collect

  • Account info: email, display name, avatar, optional handle. Used to authenticate you and power social features.
  • Location: approximate (GPS, network) — used to resolve your local community hub, suburb-scoped recipes, and food-truck pins. Never sold.
  • Pantry & recipes: ingredients you add, recipes you save, shopping lists, meal plans. Stored against your account.
  • Photos: fridge scans, recipe photos, profile pictures you upload. Stored in Supabase Storage.
  • Community content: posts, comments, channel messages, @mentions you publish in suburb hubs.
  • Device push token: an anonymous FCM token used to deliver notifications you opted into.
  • Crash & performance data: anonymous diagnostic data (via Firebase Crashlytics) so we can fix bugs.
  • Advertising ID: used by Google AdMob to serve non-personalised ads in the free tier. Reset or opt-out via your Android settings.

What we do NOT collect

  • Precise background location (we read GPS only while the app is open).
  • Contacts, SMS, call logs, microphone, or browsing history.
  • Payment card details — ChowSA is free at launch and does not process any payments. When the optional Pro tier ships in a future update, it will be billed entirely by Google Play Billing; we will receive only an entitlement status, never card details.

How we use it

  • Power the app's features (pantry, recipes, community, shopping, meal planning).
  • Deliver push notifications (mentions, shared lists, friend invites) you opted into.
  • Match your suburb to a community hub and surface localised content.
  • Detect crashes and improve stability.
  • Serve non-personalised ads in the free tier.

Who we share with

  • Supabase — our database & file hosting. Hosted in the EU. Their policy.
  • Firebase (Google) — push notifications, crash reporting, analytics. Their policy.
  • Google AdMob — non-personalised ads in the free tier. Their policy.
  • TheSportsDB — public soccer fixture API for World Cup scores. No personal data is sent.
  • Google Gemini — AI recipe and pantry matching. We send the ingredient/recipe prompt only; no account identifiers.

We never sell your personal information.

Your rights under POPIA

  • Access: request a copy of everything we hold about you — email chowsa.app.support@gmail.com.
  • Correction: edit your profile in-app, or email us.
  • Deletion: tap Settings → Privacy & Data Settings → Delete Account, OR visit our account deletion page. Removal is permanent and completes within 7 days.
  • Object: opt out of push notifications in Settings, or revoke location permission in Android settings.
  • Lodge a complaint: with the South African Information Regulator at inforegulator.org.za.

Children

ChowSA is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest in Supabase is encrypted with AES-256. Row-Level Security policies ensure only you (and people you explicitly share with) can read your data.

Changes

We'll update this page if anything changes materially and surface a notice in-app. Your continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? chowsa.app.support@gmail.com or chowsa.app.support@gmail.com for privacy-specific requests.
