Questions

Quick FAQ.

Is ChowSA free?

Yes — at launch, ChowSA is 100% free. AI meal planning, the smart pantry, vegan mode, share-to-app, the lot. No subscription, no card. An optional Pro tier may land later via Google Play Billing.

Which diets does the planner support?

Standard, Vegan, Vegetarian, Anti-inflammatory, Diabetic-friendly, Carnivore, Keto, Mediterranean, Gluten-free, Halaal, Kosher, plus a Custom prompt for anything else (allergies, religious rules, fasting windows).

How accurate are the price estimates?

Each item has an estimated Rand range based on common SA retailers. The optional "paid R__" capture per item lets you log what you actually paid — over time ChowSA builds a real, household-level price history.

Can I plan for a small household or a student budget?

Yes. Set any budget from a few hundred Rand a week and any number of days from 1 to 14. The plan and shopping list scale to fit.

What's Share-to-ChowSA?

Tap "Share" on any recipe link in Instagram, TikTok, YouTube or your browser — pick ChowSA from the share sheet. We scrape the recipe and add the ingredients to your library.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete account. Everything tied to you is wiped within 7 days, per POPIA.

Where can I get the app?

Android via Google Play (launching soon). iOS is on the roadmap.
