Questions

Quick FAQ.

Is ChowSA free?

Yes — at launch, ChowSA is 100% free with no subscription, no paywall, and no card required. An optional Pro tier may land in a future update via Google Play Billing, but for now everyone gets every feature unlocked.

How does loadshedding mode work?

ChowSA reads your suburb's current load-shedding stage and surfaces recipes tagged "Load-Shedding Friendly" (gas, coals, microwave, no-cook) when the power is off. When power's on, the full electrical menu opens up automatically.

Can I share recipes and shopping lists with my partner?

Yes. Link your partner in Settings → Partner Link. Shared shopping lists update live across both phones, and your partner sees your meal plans in real-time.

Which payment methods do you support?

None right now — ChowSA is free for everyone at launch, no payment of any kind. When the optional Pro tier ships later, it'll be billed through Google Play Billing on Android (card, Google Pay, or carrier billing — whatever you've got linked to your Play account).

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Privacy & Data Settings → Delete account. We wipe everything tied to you within 7 days, per POPIA.

Where can I get the app?

Android via Google Play (launching soon). iOS is on the roadmap.
