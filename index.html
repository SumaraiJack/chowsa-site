Questions

Quick FAQ.

Is ChowSA really free?

Yes. At launch, every feature is unlocked with no subscription, no paywall and no card. An optional Pro tier may land later via Google Play Billing — for now everyone gets everything.

Which diets does the planner support?

Standard, Vegan, Vegetarian, Anti-inflammatory, Diabetic-friendly, Carnivore, Keto, Mediterranean, Gluten-free, Halaal, Kosher, plus a Custom prompt for anything else (allergies, religious rules, fasting windows).

How accurate are the Rand price estimates?

Each item has an estimated range based on common SA retailers. The optional "paid R__" capture lets you log what you actually paid — over time ChowSA learns your real local prices.

Can I plan on a small budget?

Yes. Set any budget from a few hundred Rand and any number of days from 1 to 14. The plan and shopping list scale to fit.

What's Share-to-ChowSA?

Tap "Share" on a recipe link in Instagram, TikTok, YouTube or your browser — pick ChowSA from the share sheet. We scrape the recipe and add the ingredients to your library.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete account. Everything tied to you is wiped within 7 days, per POPIA.

Where can I get the app?

Android via Google Play (launching soon). iOS is on the roadmap.

What's the World Cup hub?

A temporary tab for live scores and matchday banter during the 2026 tournament. It'll quietly disappear after the final whistle.
